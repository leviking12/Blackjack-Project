Blackjack Rules

  • The goal is to beat the dealer's hand withought going over 21.
  • Number cards are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (jack, Queen, King) are worth 10.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever benefits the hand more.
  • you can "hit" (draw a card) or "stand" (keep your current hand).
  • If you go over 21, you "bust" and lose.
  • Dealer must hit until they reach 17 or more.
